Services

Sales

Cool Coastal supplies new and reconditioned carts and can custom build a cart to suit your needs and desires. We have gas and electric Ez-Go, Club Car and Yamaha carts. Our carts are completely inspected.

Parts

Parts available for all manufacturers: Club Car, Columbia, EZ-Go, Taylor Dunn, Yamaha to name a few. We specialize in finding your custom or hard-to-find parts.

Services

Cool Coastal repairs and refurbishes both gas and electric carts, as well as charging components. We have the know-how and tools to identify problems and put your cart back in tip-top condition.
